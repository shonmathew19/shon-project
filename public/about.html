About Me

My journey into the realm of technology began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA), where I laid the groundwork for my career. Currently, I am furthering my education by pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Applications (MCA), continuously expanding my knowledge and honing my skills to stay at the forefront of the ever-evolving tech landscape.

I have a deep love for programming and find immense satisfaction in developing websites and apps. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for problem-solving, I approach each project with enthusiasm and dedication, striving for excellence in every endeavor.

Collaborating with diverse teams, I enjoy the dynamic exchange of ideas and the opportunity to learn from others. Outside of the digital realm, I find joy in exploring the rich culture and natural beauty of Kerala, indulging in its delicious cuisine, and immersing myself in its vibrant traditions.

Feel free to reach out to me at shonformal@gmail.com to discuss potential collaborations, share insights, or simply connect over shared interests in technology and beyond. Let's innovate, automate, and create together!
